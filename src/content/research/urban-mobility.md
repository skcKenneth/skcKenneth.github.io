This programme treats movement as more than shortest distance. Current projects represent demographic constraints, energy, transfers, congestion, fairness, and uncertainty explicitly.

## Current direction

The accessible-routing and EV-dispatch studies provide the strongest technical evidence. Traffic-wave and shared-mobility experiments remain useful exploratory and teaching material.

## Availability and limitations

Code, figures, and detailed reports live in ScienceProject. Case studies should not be read as deployed transport evaluations unless the technical page identifies empirical validation.
