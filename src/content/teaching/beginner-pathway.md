Start here if you are comfortable with school mathematics but new to open-ended modeling. The pathway uses estimation, dimensional reasoning, simple recurrence and differential-equation models, spreadsheets, and introductory Python.

The central habit is to distinguish what was measured, what was assumed, and what the model actually supports.
